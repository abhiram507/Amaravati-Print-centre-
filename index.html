Why Amaravati

Printing built like a
consumer product

Every part of the Amaravati Print Centre experience — from secure upload and live preview to UPI checkout and encrypted file handling — is engineered for reliability, speed and complete peace of mind, tuned specifically for Andhra Pradesh's campuses, courts, offices and climate.

Secure upload from device

Send PDFs, Word documents and images from your phone or laptop over an encrypted, signed-in session — no cables, no shared drives, no risky USB sticks. Your file is tied to your account and never visible to anyone else.

Live preview & pricing

See a true preview of what will come out of the tray and watch the price update in real time as you change size, colour, sides, paper and binding. Reject, reorder or edit before you ever pay a rupee.

UPI-only checkout

Pay directly from PhonePe, Google Pay or Paytm. We open your chosen app with the exact order amount and merchant details pre-filled — no card entry, no cash, no wallet top-ups, no counter conversations.

Auto-wipe & privacy

Your files are stored only as long as the print job needs them and are securely shredded from every print station the moment the tray finishes. Nothing is retained, indexed or shared. Privacy is the default, not a setting.

Under 60 seconds, end-to-end

From secure upload to a warm print in the tray in less than a minute for a typical A4 document. Optimised for busy campus mornings, tender deadlines and government office rushes across Andhra Pradesh.

QR receipts & GST invoices

Every order gets a scannable QR receipt and an instant GST-compliant invoice to your email, so re-prints, expense claims and refunds are one tap away — perfect for offices, freelancers and colleges.

How it works

Four secure, transparent steps — no queue, no cash, no guesswork. From signed-in upload to warm prints in your hand in under a minute.

01

Sign in & upload securely

Sign in with your Amaravati account and upload a PDF, DOC, DOCX, PNG, JPG or WEBP from your phone or laptop over an encrypted, private session tied to you.

02

Configure & preview

Choose paper size (A4 to A1), colour or B&W, single or double-sided, paper type, binding and copies. Live pricing and a real preview update instantly.

03

Pay with UPI

Pick PhonePe, Google Pay or Paytm — your app opens with the exact amount and merchant pre-filled. Approve on your device, no cards or cash needed.

04

Collect & verify

Watch the print station whir. Collect warm prints from the tray, save the QR receipt and get a GST-compliant invoice by email — files auto-wipe when you're done.

Fair, transparent pricing

Pay only for what you print. Duplex prints get a 10% discount, premium paper and binding are transparent add-ons, and every order gets a GST-compliant invoice emailed to you instantly — no membership fees, no minimums, no surprises.
